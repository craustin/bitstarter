trip.ly is a collaborative trip-planning workspace. Trip planning is hard. It requires people management, resource management, and a devotion of time that many busy people just can't afford. This means that friends and families will discuss many trips that never actually happen because nobody steps up and takes on the work of actually planning. trip.ly will be a place for users to record their trip ideas, share them with others, collaboratively make decisions, and finally, record information on each trip participant - flight times, hotel reservations, comfirmation numbers, etc. If trip.ly is successful, users of trip.ly will take more trips after joining the site.

FAQ

How is this different from the multitude of existing travel planning sites?
When you create a trip, we won't ask you when it is, where you're going, and who you're going with. Maybe you don't know these things. Maybe you just know one of them. trip.ly should let you, at your pace, collaboratively (or on your own) think about trip ideas - and guide you to make those trip ideas become reality. We won't sell airplane tickets, host reviews, host photos, display maps of points-of-interest, help you find hotel rooms, etc.
